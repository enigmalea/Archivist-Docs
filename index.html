Invite to Server

Easy to Use

Simply invite Archivist to your Discord server and it will start recognizing links from Archive of Our Own immediately.

Info at a Glance

With a colored sidebar that shows fic rating, recognizable icons from AO3, and a preview of fanart in the fic, you can easily see important info at a glance.

Share Your Bio

Need to post an intro to a server? Did someone ask for a link to all your fics? Drop the link to your user profile, and Archivist will do the rest for you!

Get A Series Summary

Find out if a Series is complete, when it was last updated, how many works there are, and view any series notes or descriptions the authors have provided.

Advanced Features

Supports links from collections, multiple authors, multiple series, fully customizable embeds, and other convenient moderation features.
